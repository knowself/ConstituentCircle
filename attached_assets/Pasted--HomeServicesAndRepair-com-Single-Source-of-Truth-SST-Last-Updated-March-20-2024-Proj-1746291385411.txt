
# HomeServicesAndRepair.com - Single Source of Truth (SST)

*Last Updated: March 20, 2024*

## Project Overview

HomeServicesAndRepair.com is a comprehensive digital platform connecting homeowners with trusted service providers. The platform offers hyper-local service matching, real-time availability, and AI-powered service recommendations.

## Vision Statement

To become the definitive platform for home services, leveraging technology to create seamless connections between homeowners and service providers while maintaining a strong focus on local markets and community trust.

## Target Audience

- **Homeowners**: Seeking reliable home service professionals
- **Service Providers**: Local businesses looking to expand their customer base

## Revenue Model

- Premium provider listings (tiered subscriptions)
- Transaction fees from service bookings
- Value-added services for providers
- Emergency service premiums
